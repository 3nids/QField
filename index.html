Contribute

QField is an open source project, licensed under the terms of the GPLv2 or later. This means that it is free to use and modify and will stay like that. We are very happy if this app helps you to get your job done or in whatever creative way you may use it. If you found it useful, we will be even happier if you could give us something back.
